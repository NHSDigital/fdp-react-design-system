Original NHS UK Styling Formula

$conditional-border-width: $nhsuk-border-width-mobile;
$conditional-border-padding: (checkbox-size / 2) - (border-width / 2);
$conditional-margin-left: $conditional-border-padding;
$conditional-padding-left: $conditional-border-padding + $checkbox-label-padding;
